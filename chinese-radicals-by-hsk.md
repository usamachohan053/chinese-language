# List of chinese radicals by HSK

- **HSK 1:** 34 radicals
- **HSK 2:** 23 radicals
- **HSK 3:** 26 radicals
- **HSK 4:** 22 radicals
- **HSK 5:** 29 radicals
- **HSK 6:** 80 radicals
- **Total:** 214 radicals
